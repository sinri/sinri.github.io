Sinri iOS Projects :: Eden's East

So he drove out the man; and he placed at the east of the garden of Eden Cherubims, and a flaming sword which turned every way, to keep the way of the tree of life. (Genesis 3:24)

General

A third-party connect helper for VPN Gate.

Usage

The basic usage is to download and install the profile by click the button at the center of the middle tab. The profile might set up the VPN url and username and password for you. You must start to connect with this VPN by hand later.
